SEO Techniques

  1. Write Compelling Title and Description Tags

    2. A useful SEO Technique can be using compelling titles and description tags. To maximize the potential of this technique,
    it's good to use competitive keywords similar to those used in ads that appear in the SERPS. This will result in more organic clicks
    which will make your Google Ranking higher and result in your website appearing near the top of the search results.

  2. Copy Your Competitors Best Keywords

    3. Another useful SEO Technique is to emulate or create something similar to your competitors' pages that bring in the most traffic.
    By doing this you can see what is included in their pages that you can also include in yours to increase website traffic. This could
    be the type of content that's in the page, the length of the content, or even the usage of images and multimedia.

  3. Improve Your Organic CTR via title and meta description tags

    4. I found this to be very interesting because it's something we learned in class, and is pretty easy to implement into websites.
    To improve click-through rate organically, adding a relevant title tag and meta description tag to your website will boost CTR.
    Including keywords that other websites within title and description tags is essential to boosting website traffic. By using this technique
    my website ranking may improve and become more visible to the public.

SMO Techniques

  1. Use Hashtags

    2. I found the usage of hashtags to be interesting because they're the SEO of well-known social media platforms. By using hashtags,
    your content will have an increased chance of being viewed; will help the platform algorithm push your content out to people.
    Good examples of this is using #fyp in Instagram reels and TikTok video captions to get your video on the For You Page.

  2. Use Images

    3. Using images as an SMO technique is good for garnering attention from users. Images add variety and context to the content provided
    to people who visit your social media account. Images can also provide interactivity, such as Instagram posts, that would increase
    account traffic. When you post an image on Instagram, there's a comment section where people can interact with the poster and other
    commentors. I feel like images could provide more variety to my website, and therefore result in more website traffic.

  3. Create Valuable content

    4. I could apply this SMO technique to my website as it focuses on gaining attention from users via content. I would say this is the most
    valuable technique because it gives your audience value, and therefore a reason to come back and share your website/page.
    It's also proven that if content evokes emotion out of users, they're most likely to like, share and comment on your content.
